Privacy Policy

TL;DR

To put it simply:

  • I don't collect data.
  • I don't share data.
  • I don't even know what happens inside your app — everything stays on your device.

Overview

ZombiTrack respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data — period. All features, settings, and data remain entirely on your device.

Information Collection and Use

ZombiTrack does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Send data to external servers
  • Require an internet connection for normal operation
  • Display ads or use third-party SDKs

All information entered or generated in the app stays local to your device and is never shared with anyone, including the app's developer.

Data Retention

Because no data is collected, there is nothing stored outside your device and nothing retained by the developer. Deleting the app from your device removes all associated data.

Age Appropriateness

ZombiTrack contains no objectionable content and is suitable for users of all ages. There are no in-app purchases, advertisements, or social features.
